Custom Before & After Design Pricing For Each Project

Every design transformation project is unique, with different elements and deliverables that determine the final cost. Basic redesign packages typically start from $500 to $1,500, while comprehensive before & after design projects, including logo updates, visual revamps, graphics, and digital assets, can reach $3,000 or more.

The complexity of your design needs, the number of assets, and the level of customization directly impact pricing. More detailed and extensive transformations naturally require a larger investment.

Scope of Design

The range of deliverables—logos, graphics, website visuals, print materials, and social media assets—affects the overall cost. Larger scopes covering multiple design elements require more time and creative expertise.

Custom Design Elements

Tailored graphics, color schemes, and typography for your brand add value and visual appeal, influencing pricing based on complexity and quantity of assets.

Implementation & Support

Post-design support, including asset optimization, formatting, and guidance for consistent usage, ensures your brand’s visuals are applied effectively across all platforms.

How To Choose the Right Before & After Design Agency

Whether you’re giving your brand a full visual overhaul or updating specific elements, working with a design agency ensures professional guidance, expert execution, and cohesive results.

Experienced agencies apply modern design trends, visual storytelling principles, and creative frameworks to make your brand transformation impactful and memorable.

Follow the steps below to identify and select the design agency that aligns with your goals and vision for visual transformation.

Use this guide to evaluate potential agencies and choose a partner who can deliver stunning before & after designs.

Step 1: Define Your Design Needs

Clearly outline your visual transformation goals. Consider these key areas:

Visual scope: Are you refreshing select assets, creating a full before & after redesign, or updating digital and print materials?

Design style: Do you prefer minimalistic, bold, creative, or fully custom visual styles?

Objectives: Enhance brand appeal, boost engagement, modernize visuals, or increase recognition.

Deliverables: Identify what you need: before & after images, social media visuals, marketing materials, or website graphics.

Step 2: Begin Your Search

Explore portfolios, websites, and design marketplaces. Look for agencies specializing in before & after transformations with experience across industries.

Step 3: Shortlist & Evaluate Agencies

Review previous before & after projects, client feedback, and design quality. Shortlist 3–5 agencies that align with your style and project goals.

Step 4: Meet & Discuss

Meet your shortlisted agencies to discuss their creative process, tools, and approach to before & after design transformations.

Step 5: Make Your Choice

Select the agency that demonstrates creativity, technical skill, transparency, and a clear plan to execute your visual transformation effectively.
